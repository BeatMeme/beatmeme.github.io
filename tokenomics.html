Tokenomics

$BEAT is a community-driven meme token on the Sonic blockchain.

Big Picture

$BEAT lets you:

  • Trade $BEAT
  • Send $BEAT
  • Stake or farm with $BEAT

...all while enjoying the fastest L1 network. That is how we keep the hype alive.

Distribution at Launch

  • Total Supply: 100,000,000 $BEAT
  • LP (burned): 80%
  • Airdrop: 5%
  • Team Wallet: 5% + 33.3% of any fees
  • Marketing Wallet: 10% + 66.6% of any fees

No stuffy presale, no enormous dev wallet. Just a fair spread that keeps liquidity and marketing robust.

80% of the liquidity supply is burned. See proof of burn.

Trading enabled on Feb 5th, 2025 @ 00:00 UTC. See transaction.

5% of the total supply was airdropped to early supporters of the project on Arthera (before relaunch on Sonic). See airdrop transaction.

Fees & Limits

Buy Fees: 5%
Sell Fees: 5%
Max Per Transaction: 1%
Max Per Wallet: 5%

Transaction and wallet limits prevent whales from hoarding tokens and affecting the price.

Fees can never increase, only decrease. This is to ensure the community remains safe from wild fee hikes.
