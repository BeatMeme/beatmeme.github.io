# Beat Token Tokenomics

## Disclaimer

$BEAT is an experimental community token with no intrinsic value or expectation of financial return.

There is no formal team or roadmap.

## Why $BEAT?

$BEAT is an **experiment in token distribution** exploring automatic distribution of tokens to NFT holders. $BEAT is not airdropped or claimed, it automagically appears in the wallets of Arthera subscription holders.

## Tokenomics

Below is a simple breakdown of the $BEAT Token's tokenomics.

### Total Supply

1,000,000,000,000 $BEAT

### Token Distribution

#### Arthera Subscriptions Holders
- **Amount**: 500,000,000,000 $BEAT (50%)
- **Who**: Automatically allocated to the first 1,000,000 Arthera subscriptions
- **How**: 1 million valid subscription IDs (0 to 999,999), each corresponds to a claimable amount of $BEAT tokens, starting from 1 million $BEAT for ID 0 and decreasing by 1 $BEAT for each subsequent ID. For example, ID 1 can claim 999,999 $BEAT, ID 2 can claim 999,998 $BEAT, and so on, until ID 999,999 can claim 1 $BEAT.

#### Community Fund
- **Amount**: 500,000,000,000 $BEAT (50%)
- **Purpose**: To promote $BEAT and the Arthera blockchain

### Key Features

1. **No Team Allocations**: All tokens are for the community.

2. **No Presale**: Fair launch for everyone.

3. **No Taxes**: Buy, sell, or transfer without extra fees (gas fees not included).

4. **Automatic Distribution**: Tokens appear in your wallet if you hold an Arthera subscription.

5. **Community-Driven**: The community fund is intended to support the Arthera ecosystem and its projects. Tokens will be gifted to the Arthera team to allow them to determine how best to advance the ecosystem once they establish a verified multisig. In the interim, a portion of the tokens will be distributed to ecosystem projects on a best-effort basis to promote activity within the Arthera ecosystem.
